How to Delete My Account?

Thank you for using our service. If you wish to delete your account, please note the following important information:

1. Data Deletion Notice

Once your account is deleted, all related information will be removed from our database, and it will no longer be possible to search or recover your account data.

2. How to Apply

Please send an account deletion request email to conference@thehope.co, including the account information you wish to delete.

3. Important Reminders

  • After account deletion, all records and data (including user information and Workshop sessions) will be permanently removed and cannot be restored.
  • If you registered your account through a third-party service (e.g., LINE login), we will no longer access or use the information provided by the third party after the account deletion. We recommend you also stop the third-party linkage with VoiceTube.

4. Special Notes

  • Stopping the linkage with a third-party account will not delete your account information in The Hope Conference App.
  • Deleting The Hope Conference App will not delete your account information stored in our system.

Email Template

Please use the following template for your request email:

Subject: Account Deletion Request
Content:
Please assist with the deletion of my account. Registered email: _______________.
I fully understand that all account data will be permanently deleted and cannot be restored after deletion.

Processing Time

We will complete the account deletion process within five business days after receiving your request email. As this process is manually handled, we kindly ask for your understanding if there is any delay.

If you have any questions, please feel free to contact our customer support team. Thank you for your cooperation!
